Our Story

ANIK INFRA TRADING LTD (AITL) has been a prominent player in the business landscape since its establishment in 2015. While originally focusing on addressing the substantial demand and supply disparities within the transport sector, AITL has expanded its services to include the mining and logistics industries.

AITL is proud to be a Zambian-dominated company, with over 65% of its shares held by Zambian stakeholders. Our commitment to providing top-quality services to our valued clients remains unwavering. We are in a long term contract with one of the largest OEM spare parts supplier named Shore Anchor LLC, Dubai.

Empowering Zambia's Future: AITL's Commitment to Excellence, Strategic Alliances, and Indigenous Leadership in Mining and Logistics

In a significant development, AITL entered into a strategic joint venture with Ravensdale Industrial Limited. This joint venture has opened doors to a broader spectrum of mining services, including contract work, equipment repair, maintenance, rehabilitation, and parts supply (undercarriage). This collaboration has allowed us to strengthen our capabilities and enhance the services we offer, further solidifying our reputation as a dependable partner in the mining and logistics sectors. We remain dedicated to meeting the evolving needs of our clients with excellence and integrity.
